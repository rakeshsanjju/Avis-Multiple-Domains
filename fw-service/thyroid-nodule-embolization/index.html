Thyroid Nodule Embolization

  • Home
  • Services
  • Thyroid Nodule Embolization

Thyroid Nodule Embolization is a medical procedure used to treat conditions that result from excessive production of thyroid hormones such as thyroid nodules and hyperthyroidism. In this technique the arteries providing blood to thyroid gland are embolized (blocked) to ensure that hyperactive of thyroid gland is checked. It is a minimal invasive method to manage hyperthyroidism without the need for surgery.

Effectiveness of Thyroid Nodule Embolization and its applications
Thyroid Nodule Embolization is quite effective and safe method with minimally invasive procedure, sustains thyroid functioning to some extent, no scars and quick recovery – same day discharge option. Thyroid Nodule Embolization has unique applications for elderly patients unable to tolerate stress of traditional surgery and patients with significant comorbidities such as heart disease, obesity, or diabetes risky to surgical treatments.
