OUR FLAGSHIP TREATMENT

At Avis Hospitals, we proudly offer Cryo-Laser and Cryo-Sclerotherapy (CLaCS), a state-of-the-art treatment for spider veins and small varicose veins. As pioneers in this cutting-edge procedure, we are the only hospital in South India to provide CLaCS and one of the few in the entire country equipped to offer this revolutionary treatment.

CLaCS is a minimally invasive technique that combines the use of laser therapy, sclerotherapy, and cooling technology to deliver outstanding results. It is widely regarded as the best laser for spider veins on legs, offering an effective and virtually pain-free solution for patients. Additionally, this procedure incorporates augmented reality technology, which allows our specialists to visualise vein structures in real time, ensuring unparalleled accuracy during treatment. This makes CLaCS the best way to get rid of spider veins on legs with precision and speed.

The equipment used for this procedure is sourced from globally renowned manufacturers in Brazil, the USA and Israel, ensuring the highest quality and effectiveness. With its ability to treat spider veins and varicose veins safely and with minimal downtime, CLaCS is an excellent choice for those seeking advanced care.
