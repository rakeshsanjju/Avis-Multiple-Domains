What are the different treatment options available for varicose veins?

At our Andhra Pradesh varicose veins hospital, we offer advanced treatment options such as Radiofrequency Ablation (RFA) and Endovenous Laser Ablation Therapy. These minimally invasive procedures provide effective relief from the symptoms without cuts and stitches.

What is the recovery time after varicose vein treatment?

At our varicose vein hospital, the recovery time after the treatment is mostly very short; patients can walk within a few hours on the same day, and most patients can recover fully within few days and return to normal activities.

Do I need to wear compression stockings after varicose vein treatment?

Yes, wearing a compression sock after varicose vein treatment can help improve blood flow, support healing, reduce swelling, lower the risk of complications, and help in preventing the formation of new varicose veins.

Are there any risks associated with varicose vein treatment?

Our varicose veins treatments are USFDA Approved, advanced, safe, and performed by the best varicose vein specialist in Andhra Pradesh. But there are some risks such as infection at the treatment site, bruising and nerve damage.

Is varicose vein treatment covered by insurance?

At Avis Hospitals, we offer affordable varicose vein treatment in Andhra Pradesh, accepting over 10 major medical insurances to make your care more accessible and cost-effective.
